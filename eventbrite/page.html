This year, API Days SF focuses on the API Design and Strategy supporting the Internet of Things revolution. Everyone is talking about IoT: Kickstarter is overflowing with new hardware/software projects, connected objects were everywhere at the last CES. But too little time is spent talking about the underlying architecture that supports these objects, and the real challenge these companies are facing when designing their API or getting developers to use it.

API Days is a series of international and open events about APIs, the programmable web and the Platform Economy with chapters in Paris, San Francisco, Berlin, Barcelona, Moscow, Sydney. This is the 3rd edition of the SF chapter.

Following last year's successful edition on Connected Cars, we decided to expand the discussion not only to Transportation, but to IoT as a whole. Watch a recap of last year's conference: https://youtu.be/fXkF4CFD0T4?list=PLPAYnYhVjtMzyVaTldNQOg_8RSb6EY9e_

FAQs

 

What are my transport/parking options getting to the event?

All MUNI lines stop at Van Ness Station, two blocks away from PARISOMA. Take the BART to Civic Center, 7min walk. Bus lines 9, 9L, 47 at Howard St and 11th. Finally don't forget about Uber or Lyft, you are in San Francisco.

 

Where can I contact the organizer with any questions?

Please send us an email at sf@apidays.io

 

Is my registration/ticket transferrable?

Yes, please make sure you do it in advance of the event to get the right name on the pass.

 

Do I have to bring my printed ticket to the event?

Printed or mobile is fine by us, it just makes it faster to check-in everyone.

 

What is the refund policy?

Rush Tickets and Super Early Bird Tickets are non-refundable. All other tickets are refundable until June 10th 2015. In order to get a refund for your ticket, you have to issue a refund request before June 10th 2015, no request will be accepted after that date.
