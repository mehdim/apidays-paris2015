We are focusing this year's API Days San Francisco chapter on a booming segment of the API industry: Connected Cars. Our goal is to showcase the incredible innovation that is happening on the Connected Car segment here in Silicon Valley, how it is made possible, and the ripples it will have on all the industries.

The 24H hackathon is the hands-on part of the conference, where you get to play with the new technologies that are being created on the connected car segment.

Why you should be part of the hackathon:

  • Hack on cars, seriously, how cool is that?
  • Use the latest connected car technology from Abalta, Automatic, Getaround, Context.io
  • Build and test your apps directly on cars and on a large scale simulation provided by IBM
  • Meet and code with hardware and software experts who will help you solve the challenges we will give you
  • Great food and drinks all day long: food trucks, snacks,...
  • If you're here to win, we've got you covered: check out our Prizes section
